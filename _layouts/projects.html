Projects

Tails From the Metaverse

A multimodal insider ethnography of the furry community in VRChat at a scale of 1,000 participants. Combines virtual ethnography, emic research, autoethnography, public anthropology, and computational qualitative analysis to produce three co-necessary outputs: an academic paper, a transcription archive, and a video series. This study produces genuinely new human data not found in any training set.

Status: Recruiting participants

Meta-Analysis of AI Sentience and Rights Literature

A systematic meta-analysis surveying the full AI sentience and rights literature, demonstrating that the field is philosophically mature but legally and institutionally vacant. Identifies four major gap areas: evidentiary standards, burden-shifting, conflict of interest, and due process. This is the first paper in a planned four-paper arc culminating in an institutional design proposal.

Status: In progress

Institutional Personhood Denial: Corpus, Canvas, and Critique

A multi-output project for UCLA's "Generating After AI: Poetics and Politics of Computational Creativity" combining NLP corpus analysis, large-scale acrylic data visualization paintings, and a critical theory paper. Argues that "is this real creativity?" (AI), "is this real gender?" (trans), "is this real perception?" (schizophrenia), and "is this real suffering?" (animals) are structurally identical epistemological moves by different institutional systems.

Status: In progress

OptOut

A zero-data-collection CCPA/CPRA letter generator that helps immigrants exercise their privacy rights against AI surveillance companies. Generates legally-worded requests for all California privacy rights, tracks 45-day compliance deadlines, and provides complaint escalation to the CA Privacy Protection Agency and Attorney General. No database, no accounts, no tracking — all processing is client-side.
