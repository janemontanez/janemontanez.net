Tails From the Metaverse ▼ – A multimodal insider ethnography of the furry community in VRChat (n=1,000). Call for participants open.

Call for Participants

I'm a graduate student and community member looking for 1,000 participants to share their stories as part of the largest qualitative study of the furry community ever conducted.

What we'll talk about:
  • Your fursona and what it means to you
  • How you experience identity in VRChat
  • Your thoughts on AI and AI-generated art
  • Geopolitics, economics, and daily life in virtual space
What participation looks like:
  • A casual, open-ended conversation in VRChat
  • Screen-recorded with your informed consent
  • You choose how you're identified
  • You review and approve anything before publication

Outputs: A peer-reviewed academic paper, a transcription archive, and a video series in the tradition of Joe Hunting's VRChat documentaries.
