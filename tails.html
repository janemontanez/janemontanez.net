Tails From the Metaverse

A Study of the Furry Community in VRChat
Jane Montañez, JD, MLIS

Calling All VRChat Furries!

I'm a graduate student and community member looking for 1,000 participants to share their stories, experiences, and perspectives as part of the largest qualitative study of the furry community ever conducted.

What We'll Talk About
  • Your fursona and what it means to you
  • How you experience identity in VRChat
  • Your thoughts on AI and AI-generated art
  • Geopolitics, economics, and daily life in virtual space
  • What VRChat means in your everyday life
What Participation Looks Like
  • A casual, open-ended conversation in VRChat – no surveys, no Likert scales
  • Sessions are screen-recorded (avatar, voice, world context) with your informed consent
  • You choose how you're identified – your privacy is protected
  • You review and approve anything before it's published

What comes out of this? Three co-necessary outputs: a peer-reviewed academic paper, a transcription archive preserving your words in your language, and a video series in the tradition of Joe Hunting's VRChat documentaries. This study produces genuinely new human data – not scraped, not in training sets, not replicable by AI. Your voice matters.
